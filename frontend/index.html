Who We Are
Rooted in Tamil
Siddha Tradition

சித்த வளம் (Siddha Valam) was born from a deep belief that the ancient healing wisdom of Tamil Nadu deserves to reach every home. We source our herbs and formulations directly from traditional Siddha practitioners and certified organic farms across Tamil Nadu.

Every product is prepared following classical Siddha texts — the same formulations used by Tamil healers for over 5,000 years. No shortcuts. No synthetic additives. Only pure, authentic medicine.

"உடலை காக்கும் மருந்து, இயற்கையிலிருந்தே வரும்" — The medicine that protects the body comes from nature itself.
